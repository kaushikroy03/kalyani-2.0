Our Treatment Program
Patients stay in our addiction rehabilitation centre based in North 24 Parganas, West Bengal. They will participate in a 5-7 day per week program. Included in the treatment are several hours of daily therapy and treatment for their personal drug or alcohol rehabilitation. They will also receive 24 hour support from our staffs.
Program Obectives
Addiction Recovery is promoting healthy lifestyle choices that will help patients maintain life long sobriety.
The objectives of this addiction rehabilitation program are
1. support patients through the challenge of transitioning from addiction to sober living.
2. dealing with withdrawal.
3. deprogramming destructive behavior patterns.
4. building habits that will support their sobriety.
The types of treatment program are
1. Normal treatment facility - for all.
2. Special treatment facility - if asked for it.
